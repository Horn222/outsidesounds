About

I’m a lifelong professional musician, producer, and certified K–12 educator with 30+ years of experience across live performance, recording, composition, and media production. A true multi-instrumentalist, I work across percussion, strings, keyboards, and vocals—bringing authentic feel and musicality to any genre.

I’ve performed professionally since age 11 and shared stages with Grand Funk Railroad, Dave Brubeck, The Jon Spencer Blues Explosion, Starcrawler, and Andy Narell.

In the studio, I’ve worked with Steve Albini and spent decades recording, engineering, and producing—from analog tape to modern DAWs (Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Ableton Live, and more).

Now accepting new students and projects across the Tri-County Michigan area (and remote sessions by request).
