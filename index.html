About

I’m a lifelong professional musician, producer, and educator with over 30 years of experience across live performance, recording, composition, and media production. A true multi-instrumentalist, I specialize in percussion, strings, keyboards, and vocals with the ability to adapt authentically to any genre.

I’ve performed professionally since age 11 and shared stages with Grand Funk Railroad, Dave Brubeck, The Jon Spencer Blues Explosion, Starcrawler, and Andy Narell.

In the studio, I’ve worked with Steve Albini and spent decades recording, engineering, and producing music—from analog tape to modern DAWs including Logic Pro X, Pro Tools, Ableton Live, and more.

Currently accepting new projects and students throughout the Tri-County Michigan area.
